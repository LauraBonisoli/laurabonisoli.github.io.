About

I am a PhD student in Economics and Finance at the University of Verona (Mathematics and Data Analytics for Finance track). Before starting the PhD, I completed an MSc in Banking and Finance at the University of Verona, with a thesis on the intricacies of xVAs.

My current work spans two main projects: the long-term simulation of interest rate term structures through a Path-Dependent Volatility (PDV) extension of the Forward Market Model and the optimization of a life insurance product under realistic market and biometric risk constraints.

Research interests

  • Term structure modeling
  • Volatility modeling
  • Computational methods for finance
  • Risk modeling and management
  • Insurance mathematics
